AFX Dance

AFX Dance is an urban dance group founded in Fall 2011 to give dancers an opportunity to grow. Mobile Developers of Berkeley

Mobile Developers of Berkeley (MDB) is a pre-incubator for mobile apps powered by a thriving community of developers. As UC Berkeley's first and foremost mobile-focused student organization, it brings together Cal's best engineers to build the next generation of software products. Over the past 2 years, MDB has launched over 27 mobile apps in industries such as education, health, social media, shared economy, music, and location based services. It has also built the core technology behind several businesses through its contract program, and brought the Berkeley community closer to the mobile industry through its open workshops and public events.
